Julie Moon

Julie is a Korean writer and translator. She is currently a lecturer in writing at the University of California, San Diego. A former Iowa Arts Fellow and a semifinalist in the 2021 Essay Press Book Contest, she is at work on a debut essay collection. Instagram, Email
